About Us

Vero ivents was established in 2014 as a proprietary limited company which trades as Independent Living for all based in Cape Town. Independent living for all is an Orientation and Mobility company. Orientation and Mobility (O & M) is a profession specific to blindness and low vision that teaches safe, efficient and effective travel skills to people of all ages.

Objectives

The aim of this company is to provide independency to people with visual impairment and blindness.

Vision

Our vision is to become the blind and visually impaired company which provides independency to blind and visually impaired. To develop partnerships with our clients and keep abreast of technology trends to ensure that unmatched competency evolves in our services.

Mission

Our mission is to become the mobile service that will be able to reach people to their workplaces, universities, schools and private consultations. To ensure that our services are applied in the best interest of our clients

Values

  • To be transparent and being honest to our customers
  • To act with integrity, to be accountable for the highest standards of behavior, including honesty and fairness in all aspects of our work traffic lights training
  • To understand the traffic cycle for them to be able to know when to cross

Why us

Why use Independent living for all as your orientation and mobility partner?

We are a mobile professional company that is willing to make changes in peoples’ lives. As we are all aware that the technology and systems changes every day in workplaces, universities, schools and private consultations, blind people also need to be updated even the previously trained people need to be revised to the new systems.
